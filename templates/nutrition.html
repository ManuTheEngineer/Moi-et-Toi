When We Disagree
1Pause. Take 20 minutes apart to cool down. No texting in anger.
2Own your part. Start with "I feel ___ when ___." No blame.
3Listen to understand, not to respond. Repeat back what you heard.
4Find the need. Behind every complaint is an unmet need. Find it.
5Repair. Say what you'll do differently. Mean it. Follow through.
