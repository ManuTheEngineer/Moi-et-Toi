Foundation

Your Sculpt Guide

Strength · Shape · Confidence

This plan is designed for beginners. Every move has clear timing and built-in rest. Go at your own pace. Showing up is what matters.
Phase 1
Core Strengthening
01

Lying Leg Raises

Lie flat. Raise straight legs to ceiling, lower without touching floor. Go slow. The slower, the better.

⏱ 10 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 45s rest
02

Flutter Kicks

Hands under hips, legs a few inches off ground. Small alternating kicks. Lower back pressed down.

⏱ 20 sec↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
03

Reverse Crunches

Knees bent, curl hips off floor bringing knees to chest. Hold 2 sec at top, lower gently.

⏱ 12 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 45s rest
04

Mountain Climbers

Push-up position. Drive knees to chest alternating. Like running on the floor. Keep hips level.

⏱ 25 sec↻ 3 sets☁ 50s rest
05

Dead Bugs

On back, arms up, knees 90 degrees. Extend opposite arm and leg slowly. Return, switch sides.

⏱ 8 each side↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
Phase 2
Waist Sculpting
06

Russian Twists

Seated, lean back, feet off ground. Rotate torso side to side. Controlled. No rushing.

⏱ 16 reps (8/side)↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
07

Side Plank Hip Dips

Side plank on forearm. Dip hips down and back up. Feel it along the waistline.

⏱ 10 each side↻ 3 sets☁ 45s rest
08

Standing Side Crunches

Hand behind head. Lift knee to meet same-side elbow. Squeeze at top. No equipment needed.

⏱ 12 each side↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
09

Woodchoppers

Feet apart. Diagonal chopping motion from shoulder to opposite hip. Tightens the waist beautifully.

⏱ 10 each side↻ 3 sets☁ 45s rest
Every Day
Your Daily Practice
✦ Do This Every Day

Stomach Vacuums

Breathe out completely. Pull belly button toward spine. Hold 15 to 20 seconds. Repeat. Trains your deepest core muscle. Like an internal corset.

⏱ 15-20s hold↻ 5 rounds☁ 15s rest
Phase 3
Shape & Silhouette
10

Hip Thrusts

Back against couch. Feet flat, knees bent. Drive hips up, squeeze 2 seconds at top.

⏱ 15 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 50s rest
11

Sumo Squats

Wide stance, toes out. Squat deep, chest up. Press through heels to stand.

⏱ 15 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 50s rest
12

Lateral Shoulder Raises

Light weights at sides. Raise arms to shoulder height. Lower with control. Shapes the silhouette.

⏱ 12 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest

Elevated

Elevated Sculpt

Power · Definition · Shape

You made it here because you put in the work. Higher reps, shorter rest, more intense combinations. Trust the process. You've already proven you can do this.
Phase 1
Deep Core Sculpt
01

Hanging Leg Raises

Hang from bar. Raise straight legs to 90 degrees. Lower with full control.

⏱ 12 reps↻ 4 sets☁ 30s rest
02

Scissor Kicks

Legs hovering, cross over each other in X pattern. Core tight. Lower legs = harder.

⏱ 40 sec↻ 4 sets☁ 25s rest
03

Plank to Pike

Plank position. Walk feet to hands, hips into inverted V. Walk back out. One fluid motion.

⏱ 12 reps↻ 4 sets☁ 30s rest
04

V-Ups

Lie flat. Lift legs and upper body simultaneously, reach for toes. Lower with control.

⏱ 15 reps↻ 4 sets☁ 30s rest
05

Mountain Climber Sprints

As fast as you can. Explosive knee drives. Full intensity cardio + core.

⏱ 45 sec↻ 4 sets☁ 25s rest
Phase 2
Waist Definition
06

Weighted Russian Twists

Hold a dumbbell or heavy water bottle. Added resistance sculpts deeper waist definition.

⏱ 24 reps (12/side)↻ 4 sets☁ 25s rest
07

Side Plank Reach Through

Side plank. Thread top arm underneath body, rotating torso. Return. Sharpens the waistline.

⏱ 12 each side↻ 4 sets☁ 25s rest
08

Band Woodchoppers

Resistance band at head height. Pull diagonally across body. Controlled rotation.

⏱ 14 each side↻ 4 sets☁ 30s rest
09

Slow Bicycle Crunches

Elbow to opposite knee. Hold each twist 1 second. Slow is where results live.

⏱ 20 reps (10/side)↻ 4 sets☁ 25s rest
Phase 3
Power Sculpt
10

Weighted Hip Thrusts

Weight across hips. Press up, squeeze 3 seconds at top. Number one curve builder.

⏱ 15 reps↻ 4 sets☁ 35s rest
11

Bulgarian Split Squats

One foot on couch behind you. Lower until front thigh is parallel. Press back up.

⏱ 12 each leg↻ 4 sets☁ 35s rest
12

Arnold Press

Dumbbells at chest, palms facing you. Press up rotating palms outward. Full shoulder sculpt.

⏱ 12 reps↻ 4 sets☁ 30s rest

Full Body

Full Body Sculpt

Head to Toe · One Session

Every major muscle group in one workout. Upper body, core, waist, glutes, legs. Allow 60 to 75 minutes. Warm up 5 minutes first. You've got this.
Block A
Upper Body
01

Push-Ups

Shoulder-width hands. Lower chest to floor, push back up. Drop to knees if needed.

⏱ 10 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
02

Lateral Raises

Light weights. Raise arms to shoulder height. Lower slowly. Builds shoulder caps.

⏱ 15 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 35s rest
03

Bent Over Rows

Hinge forward. Pull weights to ribs, squeeze shoulder blades. Strengthens the back.

⏱ 12 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
04

Tricep Dips

Edge of chair. Lower by bending elbows. Push back up. Tones back of arms.

⏱ 12 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
Block B
Core + Waist
05

Leg Raises

Raise straight legs, lower without touching floor. Lower back pressed down.

⏱ 12 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 35s rest
06

Bicycle Crunches

Elbow to opposite knee. Go slow. Hold each twist 1 second.

⏱ 20 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 35s rest
07

Russian Twists

Seated, lean back, feet off ground. Rotate side to side. Controlled and steady.

⏱ 20 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 35s rest
08

Plank Hold

Forearms on ground, body straight. Hold. Breathe. Don't let hips sag.

⏱ 30-45 sec↻ 3 sets☁ 30s rest
Block C
Lower Body + Glutes
09

Sumo Squats

Wide stance, toes out. Squat deep. Hold weight at center for extra challenge.

⏱ 15 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
10

Hip Thrusts

Back against couch. Drive hips up, squeeze at top 2 to 3 seconds.

⏱ 15 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
11

Reverse Lunges

Step back, lower knee toward ground. Push through front heel. Alternate legs.

⏱ 12 each leg↻ 3 sets☁ 40s rest
12

Glute Bridges

On back, knees bent. Lift hips, squeeze glutes at top. Hold 2 seconds.

⏱ 15 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 35s rest
13

Calf Raises

Rise onto toes, hold 1 second, lower slowly. Completes the leg sculpt.

⏱ 20 reps↻ 3 sets☁ 30s rest
